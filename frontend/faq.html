Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. Enter your email, create a strong password, and verify your email address. You can then complete your profile and start trading.

How do I verify my identity?

Go to Settings → Verification. Upload a government-issued ID and proof of address. Verification usually takes 24-48 hours.

What is the minimum trade amount?

The minimum trade amount varies by pair. For BTC/USDT it's 0.0001 BTC, for ETH/USDT it's 0.001 ETH, and for USDT pairs it's 1 USDT.

How does leverage work?

Leverage allows you to trade with more than your account balance. For example, with 10x leverage, a $100 margin gives you $1,000 buying power. Higher leverage increases both potential profits and risks.

What is a funding rate?

Funding rates are periodic payments between long and short traders to keep the perpetual contract price close to the spot price. Rates are calculated every 8 hours.

How do I enable 2FA?

Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. Choose between Google Authenticator or SMS verification and follow the setup instructions.

What is an anti-phishing code?

An anti-phishing code is a unique word or number that appears in all legitimate emails from Ever Finance. If you receive an email without this code, it's likely a phishing attempt.

What are the trading fees?

Spot trading fees are 0.10% for makers and 0.15% for takers. Futures fees are 0.02% for makers and 0.05% for takers. VIP tiers offer discounted rates based on 30-day volume.

Are there withdrawal fees?

Yes, withdrawal fees cover network transaction costs. Fees vary by cryptocurrency and network conditions. Current fees are displayed on the withdrawal page.

How do I use the API?

Go to API Management to create API keys. You can set permissions for reading, trading, and withdrawals. Our API documentation has examples in Python, JavaScript, and other languages.

What is the order types?

We support Limit, Market, Stop-Limit, Stop-Market, and Trailing Stop orders. Advanced orders are available for futures and margin trading.
