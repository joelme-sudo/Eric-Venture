Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. You can then complete your profile and start trading.

What are the trading fees?

Our fees start at 0.10% for makers and 0.15% for takers. VIP tiers offer lower fees based on 30-day trading volume. Check our Fees page for details.

How do I enable 2FA?

Go to Security Settings, click "Enable 2FA", and follow the instructions to set up Google Authenticator or SMS verification.

How long do withdrawals take?

Crypto withdrawals are usually processed within minutes. Bank withdrawals take 1-3 business days depending on your bank.
